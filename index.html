country hotel

Country Hotel is a small family run hotel, dedicated to offering our guests the best the Yorkshire Dales have to offer - top quality produce and cooking, warm welcoming hospitality, and a relaxing and homely place to rest and recharge. Owned and run by the Winters family since 2006, our focus is on providing you with beautiful bedroom suites, inside the comfortable and spacious country hotel, to base yourself while exploring the Yorkshire dales region.

Situated near the village of Keld, we are surrounded by the breathtaking scenery of the Yorkshire Dales National Park. We offer peace and quiet, invigorating adventure, or merely the chance to relax and explore our charming villages and their traditional way of life. The hotel is ideally located, with many activities and a large array of walks from our front door.

Our highly popular restaurant offers first class food using the best locally sourced ingredients. We have partnered with many local artisan producers who provide high quality products with pride and dedication.

We hope you will visit us soon as we look forward to welcoming you.
